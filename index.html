What is self idenity?:
Self-identity is recognition of a person characteristics as an individual, this also ties into what identity is and why identity is important
What is identity?:
The fact of being who or what a person or thing is. The way that we define identity is our sense of who we are as individuals and/or members of social groups and society.
Why is identity important?:
Identity is important as having an identity can give a sense of belonging and helps wellbeing and confidence. Which can then help you with making friends with like-minded people. Identity is important as it also helps you feel like yourself and a unique individual.
How to deal/move on from an identity crisis?:
The best way to deal with an identity crisis is by talking to a therapist as deep internal questioning, and self reflection. This will help you with your identity crisis and help your mental health problems that have occurred due to said identity crisis
How to form a relationship with idenity?:
The way to form a relationship with identity is to get to know another person and to understand them, understand thier body language and the way they act and to share similar experiences.
Why is self-identity important for individuals?:
When someone has awareness of their self identity, they better understand themselves, their ambitions and their goals as a whole, self identity also helps them understand and form thier own standards, values and morals.
