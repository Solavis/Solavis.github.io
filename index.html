What is self idenity?:
Self-identity is recognition of a person characteristics as an individual, this also ties into what identity is and why identity is important
What is identity?:
The fact of being who or what a person or thing is. The way that we define identity is our sense of who we are as individuals and/or members of social groups and society.
Why is identity important?:
Identity is important as having an idenityt can give a sense of belonging and helps wellbeing and confidence. Which can then help you with making friends with like-minded people. Identity is important as it also helps you feel like yourself and a unique individual.
How to deal/move on from an identity crisis?:
The best way to deal with an identity crisis is by talking to a therapist as deep internal questioning, and self reflection. This will help you with your identity crisis and help your mental health problems that have occured due to said identity crisis
